How to improve your curving

Simple step-by-step guidance on how to make your curving work, at least a bit...

This procedure will help you improve your curving technique if you have already tried it and know the basic idea behind it, but when on the slope, you are still somehow unable to perform it well. To get familiar with the concept of curving technique itself, please find the another topic.
  1. Don't start on some easy peasy slope to train a bit. Instead, pick the blackest slope you see around you, as curving simply work only from a certain degree of tilt.
  2. Start skiing, gain some speed, but keep your curves well-managed and long-shaped.
  3. Stop trying to work out your curves only by thee work of your legs and knees. Instead. try to incline your whole body on the slope surface, while keeping the axis of your body as straight as possible.
  4. Push your body closer and closer to the surface with every new curve. In the end, imagine that you want to slap the slope with your elbow.
  5. If you succeed in it, you will definitely feel the edges of your ski cut into the snow when curving...
