How to start skiing

Step-by-step guidance on how to start skiing for the first time.

These guidelines are meant for complete beginners as they are focused on taking on ski equipment. If you are already familiar with such procedures and want to improve your skiing skills, search for other topics.
  1. Prepare all the necessary skiing equipments:
    • ski
    • ski poles
    • ski boots
    • helmet
    • ski goggles
    • gloves
  2. Take on your ski boots.
  3. Check that the binding is in the step mode.
  4. Put your ski boot into the binding.
  5. Push the tip of your boot to the front part of the binding and click the heel to the back part of the binding.
    Note:
    Don't hesitate to use some force.
  6. Click the tip of your boot to the front part of the binding.
    Note:
    You can use your hand to hold the ski and pre-open the binding.
  7. Position your boot into the binding and click the lock up your heel by your hand.
  8. Put on the ski goggles.
  9. Put the gloves on.
  10. Take on the helmet.
  11. Grab the poles.
  12. Go to the slope track ski jump and start.
